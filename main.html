My Story

Hi there! I'm Josh. I'm currently enrolled in the full time coding bootcamp at The Georgia Institute of Technology. After high school, I spent time at Kennesaw State and eventually made my way to The University of Georgia. While I greatly enjoyed my time as a student, I began to discover that I was in school for the entirely wrong reasons. I gave it the old college try but wound up dropping out. I fell into a job doing GIS analysis. What began as a job doing monotonous mapping projects quickly led to implementing SQL server and creating data driven platforms.

The thought of learning SQL was overwhelming at first. It looked like a bunch of nonsense. However, upon some further digging I discovered that something that looked incredibly complex at first actually became quite simple to me... which leads me to my current adventure.

Business aside, you can find me in Sanford Stadium on any given Saturday that the Dawgs play at home. I have seen every episode of The Office AT LEAST 10 times. There is no amount of hot wings that I will not try to tackle. I have a wonderful girlfriend and family that are the highlight of my life.
